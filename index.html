Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know before installing.

Is AllAdsBlock really free?

Yes. The Free plan blocks ads, video ads, pop-ups, and malware sites with no time limit, no credit card, and no account. Pro and Ultimate add privacy and security features for a monthly fee.

Does it collect my browsing data?

No. AllAdsBlock does not track what sites you visit, collect browsing history, or send personal data to us. All blocking runs locally in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does it block video ads on streaming sites?

Yes. AllAdsBlock blocks and auto-skips video ads on many streaming and news sites — including pre-roll and mid-roll ads that play before or during videos.

Will it slow down my browser?

No — it speeds it up. AllAdsBlock uses Chrome's modern Manifest V3 (MV3) architecture with native browser rules. Fewer ads means fewer resources downloaded, so pages actually load faster.

What happens if a site detects my ad blocker?

Pro and Ultimate include anti-adblock bypass, which hides the extension from detection scripts on sites that would otherwise nag or block you.

Can I pause it on specific sites?

Yes. Click the AllAdsBlock icon in your Chrome toolbar and press "Pause on this site" to allow ads on any site you choose to support.

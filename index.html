Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know before installing.

Is AllAdsBlock really free?

Yes. The Free plan blocks ads, video ads, pop-ups, and malware sites with no time limit, no credit card, and no account needed. The Pro and Ultimate plans add privacy and security features for a monthly fee.

Does it collect my browsing data?

No. AllAdsBlock does not track what sites you visit, collect browsing history, or send any personal data to us. All blocking decisions happen locally in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Does it block video ads on streaming sites?

Yes. AllAdsBlock blocks and auto-skips video ads on many streaming and news sites — including pre-roll and mid-roll ads that play before or during videos.

Will it slow down my browser?

No — it speeds it up. AllAdsBlock is built on Chrome's modern Manifest V3 (MV3) architecture, which uses native browser rules instead of intercepting every request. Blocking ads means fewer resources to download, so pages actually load faster.

What happens if a site detects my ad blocker?

The Pro and Ultimate plans include anti-adblock bypass, which hides the extension from detection scripts on sites that would otherwise block or nag you.

Can I pause it on specific sites?

Yes. Click the AllAdsBlock icon in your Chrome toolbar and press "Pause on this site" to temporarily allow ads on any site you choose to support.

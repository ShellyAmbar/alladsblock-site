What it does

🚫 Block ads & pop-ups

Banner ads, pop-ups, pop-unders, and video (VAST/VMAP) ads across the web — including stubborn anti-adblock sites.

🔒 Stop trackers

Blocks Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and dozens of analytics and ad-exchange trackers; strips tracking parameters from links.

🛡️ Block dangerous sites

Known malware domains are blocked using a live threat feed (URLhaus), with a full-page safety warning — plus phishing-lookalike and brand-impersonation detection.

🔑 Protect your logins

Warns and blocks when a password would be sent over insecure HTTP or to a different domain, and flags brand-impersonation lookalikes.

🕵️ Anti-fingerprinting

Canvas-noise and plugin masking reduce how easily sites can fingerprint and track you across the web.

⛏️ Block cryptominers

Stops in-browser cryptomining scripts that silently drain your CPU and battery.
